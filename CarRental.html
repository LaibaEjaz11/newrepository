FAQ'S

Can I specify the brand and car model I want?

Unfortunately, suppliers don't generally provide the specific brand or model of their cars, but you can see each car's type when searching. Please bear in mind that different cities and regions may categorize cars in different ways, and the car you receive at pick-up is subject to the availability at the branch.

What is covered in the basic rental price?

The basic price includes the rental fee for the period you're using the car, as well as associated taxes and fees. For details, please refer to the breakdown on the booking details page.

What documents do I need to bring when I pick up the car?

When you pick up your car, you'll need the following: 1. Your pick-up voucher. 2. The original copy of your license (which must meet the rental company's requirements), and a valid ID. 3. A credit card with sufficient balance (you may need to provide 2 cards for some higher end car models). The rental company will preauthorize the deposit amount.

What is the cancellation policy?

If you cancel your booking before the free cancellation deadline, you'll receive a full refund. After this time, you'll need to pay a fee if you cancel. Once you've picked up your car, you won't be able to cancel your booking. You also won't be able to cancel if you don't show up to pick up your car at the designated time (this is sometimes called a "no show").

If I don't purchase roadside assistance but later need it when I'm driving, what should I do?

When you pick up the car, the rental branch will give you a receipt or a rental contract. This will usually include a contact number for roadside assistance, which you can use to request help. If you can't find this number, we recommend contacting the branch and following their advice. The cost of roadside assistance varies depending on the circumstances of the accident or issue.

What's the fuel policy for the car I rented?

Most rental companies have their own fuel policy. It's a good idea to check the details at the branch when you pick up your car.
